Treatment Can Make a Difference

Treatment Options (for ESRD) Dialysis: the process of removing a patient’s blood from the body, filtering it, and putting it back in them. 50% death rate after 5 years 12-15 hours a week Mimics 10-15% normal kidney function

Transplant: surgically transfer another person’s organ or tissue into a patient. Average kidney transplant lasts 9-14 years Immunosuppression 3-5 year wait (5-10 in California) and dietary changes to keep off of dialysis- and even eliminate the need for it entirely.
